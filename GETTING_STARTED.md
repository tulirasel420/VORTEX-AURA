## ❓ Common Questions

**Q: What broker should I use with QUANTEX-BOT?**  
A: Currently optimized for **Quotex**. IQ Option pair names are also supported in the Future Engine.

**Q: What is M1 trading?**  
A: M1 means 1-minute candles. You open a trade and it expires in 1 minute. QUANTEX-BOT generates M1 signals.

**Q: How accurate are the signals?**  
A: Signal accuracy varies by market conditions. During strong trends, win rates of 70-85% are common. During sideways/choppy markets, the bot will produce fewer signals to maintain quality.

**Q: What is MTG?**  
A: Martingale. If you lose a trade, the next trade amount is doubled to recover the loss plus make profit. Use carefully with proper money management.

**Q: Can I run multiple pairs at once?**  
A: Yes, in Auto Mode the bot monitors all pairs simultaneously and sends the strongest signal from any pair.

**Q: What happens if I close Telegram?**  
A: The bot runs on the server. Your session continues running. You'll still receive all signal notifications.
