## 🔄 MTG (Martingale)

### What is MTG?
MTG stands for **Martingale**. If your first signal results in a LOSS, the bot activates MTG mode and looks for a recovery trade where you double your stake to recover the loss and make profit.

### Should I always use MTG?
MTG is powerful for recovery but doubles your risk. Recommended money management:
- Base stake: 1–2% of your balance
- MTG stake: 2–4% of your balance (2x base)
- Maximum 1 MTG level (don't do multiple recoveries)

### Is MTG automatic?
The bot automatically **analyzes and sends** the MTG signal when conditions are met. Whether you actually place the MTG trade is your choice.
